Getting started

How do I play?

  • Open the app and pick any mini-game from the home screen.
  • Each game starts with a short how-to-play card, then a quick round.
  • Play at your own pace — there is no penalty for taking your time.

What is the brain-age test?

During onboarding you can take a brief tapping test and enter your age. BrainFit uses your speed and accuracy, anchored to the age you entered, to show a light-hearted "brain age" estimate. You can retake it any time — it is meant for fun and motivation, not as a measurement of your health.

How accurate is the brain age?

The brain-age number and game scores are for-fun estimates only. They are not validated, not a clinical measure, and can vary between attempts depending on your focus, the device, and the moment. Never treat a result as a diagnosis. See our Terms of Use for the full disclaimer.

Subscriptions & billing

What does BrainFit Pro include?

Some games are free with daily limits, and the onboarding brain test is free. BrainFit Pro is an optional auto-renewing subscription that unlocks unlimited training. The yearly plan may include a 3-day free trial.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are billed through your Apple ID. Manage or cancel any time in Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions on your iPhone, or at apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions. If you started a free trial, cancel at least 24 hours before it ends to avoid being charged. Deleting the app does not cancel a subscription.

How do I restore a purchase?

If you reinstalled the app or switched devices, open the upgrade screen and tap Restore Purchases while signed in with the same Apple ID you used to subscribe.

How do I request a refund?

All purchases are processed by Apple, so refunds are handled by Apple at reportaproblem.apple.com. We aren't able to issue App Store refunds directly.

Privacy & your data

BrainFit has no account or login, and we don't ask for your name, email, or phone number. Your settings, brain-age result, and training streak are stored locally on your device. We collect only anonymized usage and crash analytics to improve the app — no ads, no IDFA tracking, no data sales. To remove all on-device data, simply delete the app. Full details are in our Privacy Policy.
