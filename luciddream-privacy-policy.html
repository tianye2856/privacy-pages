Privacy Policy & Terms of Use

Last updated: June 25, 2026 · Version 1.0

Lucid Dream Atlas helps you record dreams and receive AI-generated dream interpretations for self-reflection and cultural curiosity.

Dream interpretations are for personal reflection, journaling, and cultural exploration. They are not medical, psychological, legal, financial, religious, or professional advice, and they do not predict future events.

1. What the App Does

Lucid Dream Atlas lets users record dream text, choose interpretation lenses, and receive AI-generated interpretations that may use symbolic, cultural, or psychological perspectives based on the information provided in the app.

2. Information We Collect

The app may process information you choose to provide, including:

  • Dream text you enter.
  • Emotion labels and interpretation lens choices.
  • Onboarding answers such as age range, country, occupation, recall habits, recurring dream symbols, and interpretation preferences.
  • App language or system language settings used to display the interface.

We do not require your real name, email address, phone number, or account credentials to use the basic dream interpretation flow.

3. How We Use Information

We use information you provide to:

  • Generate dream interpretations.
  • Personalize interpretation style, language, and cultural context.
  • Display recent dream entries and related in-app content.
  • Improve the user experience and maintain the app.

We do not sell your dream content or personal information.

4. AI Processing

To generate interpretations, the app may send your dream text and related context to a third-party AI model provider. The provider processes submitted content to return an interpretation.

Please do not enter highly sensitive personal information, passwords, government ID numbers, financial account details, health records, or content you do not want processed by an AI service.

5. Data Storage

The app may store recent dream entries, onboarding preferences, and interpretation results locally on your device. Local data may be removed if you delete the app or clear app data. When you request an AI interpretation, the dream text and context needed for that request are sent to the AI processing service.

6. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your information. We may disclose information only when necessary to:

  • Provide the AI interpretation service.
  • Comply with applicable law or legal process.
  • Protect the safety, rights, or integrity of users, the app, or others.
