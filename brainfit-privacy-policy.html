Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026 · Version 1.0

BrainFit is a brain-training mini-games app for older adults. This page explains exactly what data we touch, where it goes, and your choices. Short version: no account, no ads, no cross-app tracking, no data sales — your settings and brain-age result stay on your device.

Health note. BrainFit is for entertainment and motivation only. It is not a medical device and does not diagnose or treat any condition. See our Terms of Use for the full disclaimer.

1. Who we are

BrainFit (脑力时光) is an iOS app distributed through the Apple App Store. In this policy, "we", "our", and "us" refer to the operator of BrainFit; "you" means anyone who installs or uses the app. Contact: feedback@globalpixelstudio.com.

2. Data you give us

No account is required and we do not ask for your name, email, phone number, or contacts. During onboarding you may enter your age and pick a text size; this is used on-device to compute your for-fun brain-age estimate and to size the interface. We do not receive this as identifiable data.

3. Data collected automatically

To understand which features are used and to keep the app stable, we collect anonymized usage analytics and crash diagnostics through Google Firebase. This may include: app screens viewed, games started and completed, button taps within the funnel, app version, device model, OS version, language, coarse region, and crash logs.

No tracking, no ads. We do not use the Advertising Identifier (IDFA), we do not serve ads, and we do not track you across other apps or websites. Analytics are used only to improve BrainFit and are not linked to your identity.

4. Data stored on your device

Your settings (language, text size), your brain-age result, and your training streak are stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults. This stays on your device and is removed if you delete the app.

5. Subscriptions & payments

Purchases of BrainFit Pro are processed entirely by Apple through your Apple ID. We never see or store your payment card or full Apple ID. We receive only the anonymized entitlement status (whether a subscription is active) needed to unlock features. Apple's handling of payments is governed by Apple's privacy policy.

6. Third-party services

  • Google Firebase (Analytics & Crashlytics-style diagnostics). Processes the anonymized usage and crash data above. See Firebase privacy and Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple. Distribution, in-app purchases, and (if you opt in) crash analytics via App Store. See Apple's Privacy Policy.

7. Data retention

Anonymized analytics are retained by Firebase for a limited period per Google's defaults and then aggregated or deleted. On-device data persists until you delete the app.

8. Your rights

Depending on where you live (e.g., GDPR in the EU/UK, CCPA in California), you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data, and to object to certain processing. Because we collect only anonymized analytics and store personal preferences locally, the simplest way to remove all on-device data is to delete the app. For other requests, email feedback@globalpixelstudio.com. We do not sell personal information.

9. Children's privacy

BrainFit is designed for adults (with older adults in mind) and is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent age in your region). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. International transfers

Our service providers (e.g., Google, Apple) may process data on servers outside your country. Where required, appropriate safeguards apply to such transfers.

11. Security

We rely on Apple's platform protections and reputable providers. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your information using industry-standard practices.

12. Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected by updating the date above. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.

13. Contact us

Email: feedback@globalpixelstudio.com

We try to reply within five business days.
