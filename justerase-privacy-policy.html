Just Erase — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Just Erase app ("Just Erase", "we", "us") handles information when you use it to remove unwanted people and objects from your photos. We built Just Erase to do one job well, and we keep data collection to what that job needs.

1. Photos you edit

You choose each photo you want to edit. Photos are processed only to produce the edit you asked for. Simple erases run on your device. When you choose a higher-quality AI clean-up, the selected image and the area you brushed are sent to our processing provider, the result is returned to you, and the image is not retained after processing completes. We do not use your photos to train models, and we never sell them.

2. Information we collect

  • App usage & diagnostics — anonymous events (for example, an edit finished, a feature was opened, a crash occurred) to keep the app stable and decide what to improve.
  • Subscription status — whether you have an active subscription, managed through Apple. We never see your full payment details.
  • Device & technical data — app version, device model, and OS version, attached to diagnostics.

We do not ask for your name, email, contacts, or location.

3. Service providers

We use a small number of third parties strictly to operate the app: cloud image-processing for the AI clean-up feature, and analytics/crash tools (such as Firebase). These providers process data only on our behalf and under their own privacy terms. Purchases and subscriptions are handled by Apple.

4. Data retention

Uploaded images for cloud clean-up are processed transiently and not stored by us afterward. Anonymous diagnostics are retained only as long as needed to operate and improve the app.

5. Children

Just Erase is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their data.

6. Your choices

You can stop all data collection by deleting the app. You can manage or cancel your subscription anytime in your Apple Account settings. To request deletion of any diagnostic data associated with your device, contact us below.

7. Changes

We may update this policy as the app evolves; the "Last updated" date above will change accordingly.

8. Contact
