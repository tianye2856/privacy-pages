Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026 · Version 1.0

CaseCrack is an offline detective deduction puzzle game for iPhone. The current version does not require an account, does not collect data, does not show ads, and does not track you across apps or websites.

1. Who we are

This policy applies to the iOS app CaseCrack. In this policy, "we", "our", and "us" refer to the operator of CaseCrack. Contact: feedback@globalpixelstudio.com.

2. Data we collect

Current version: CaseCrack does not collect data from the app.

We do not ask you to create an account and we do not collect your name, email address, phone number, precise location, contacts, photos, camera input, microphone input, health data, advertising identifier, gameplay analytics, diagnostics, or crash reports through a third-party SDK.

3. Data stored on your device

CaseCrack stores your selected language, completed cases, scores, grades, hint counts, and streaks locally on your device using iOS local storage. This information stays on your device and is not sent to us.

You can remove local progress by using the reset option in the app or by deleting the app from your device.

4. Tracking, ads, and analytics

CaseCrack does not use the App Tracking Transparency framework, IDFA, third-party advertising SDKs, attribution SDKs, cross-app tracking, or analytics SDKs in the current version.

5. Sharing

CaseCrack includes a system share action for your result text. Sharing is handled by iOS and the destination app you choose. CaseCrack does not receive or store that shared data.

6. Purchases and accounts

The current version does not include subscriptions, in-app purchases, advertising, account creation, cloud sync, leaderboards, or online multiplayer features.

7. Children's privacy

CaseCrack is a general-audience puzzle game and is not directed to children under 13 or the equivalent minimum age in your region. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. International transfers

Because the current version does not collect data from the app, we do not transfer app-collected personal data internationally.

9. Your choices

Since CaseCrack stores progress locally, deleting the app removes the local app data from your device. You can also contact us with privacy questions at feedback@globalpixelstudio.com.

10. Changes

If a future version adds accounts, online features, analytics, purchases, advertising, or any other data collection, we will update this policy and the App Store privacy information before release.

11. Contact us

Email: feedback@globalpixelstudio.com
