Quick help

The erase didn't look clean. Try brushing a little wider around the object so the app has room to rebuild, or switch to the higher-quality AI clean-up for tricky areas.

How do I export at full quality? Full-resolution export is included with Premium; tap the export button after your edit.

Subscriptions

Premium unlocks unlimited erases, the sharpest background rebuild, and full-resolution exports. You can manage or cancel anytime in your Apple Account: Settings → your name → Subscriptions, or at apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions. Subscriptions renew automatically unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the period.
