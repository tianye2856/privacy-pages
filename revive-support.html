Support

Last updated: June 29, 2026

Help for restoring old photos, colorizing, unblurring, subscriptions, and privacy in Revive.

Contact us

Email feedback@globalpixelstudio.com for support, privacy questions, subscription issues, or feature requests. We usually reply within 1–2 business days.

Restoring a photo

  • Tap to pick an old, blurry, scratched, or black-and-white photo (or take a photo of a printed picture).
  • Choose what you want: repair damage, restore faces, colorize, unblur & upscale to HD, or animate.
  • Wait a few seconds while the photo is processed, then compare before/after.
  • Save the result to your photo library or share it.

Tips for the best results

  • Start from the clearest scan or copy you have; better input gives better output.
  • For very damaged photos, try repair first, then colorize or upscale.
  • Results are AI-generated estimates — colors and reconstructed details are interpretations and may differ from the original.

Subscriptions

Some features (such as high-resolution export and animation) require an optional auto-renewable subscription handled by Apple. To manage or cancel:

  • Open Settings on your iPhone.
  • Tap your Apple ID name.
  • Tap Subscriptions.
  • Select Revive and manage the plan.

Refund requests are handled by Apple at reportaproblem.apple.com. To restore a previous purchase, use "Restore Purchases" in the app.

Privacy

Only the photos you select are sent for processing. Read the full Privacy Policy and Terms of Use for details on how images are handled.

Troubleshooting

  • Restoration needs an internet connection — make sure you are online.
  • If you cannot pick a photo, check that Revive has Photos permission in Settings › Privacy.
  • If products or subscriptions do not load, check your App Store account and try again later.
  • If the app behaves unexpectedly, restart it and confirm you are on the latest version from the App Store.
