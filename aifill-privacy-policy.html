Last updated: May 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Global Pixel Studio ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use AI Replace & Remove - Fill App, also shown in the app as AI Fill (package name: com.skysoft.aifill).

By using the app, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the app.

Information We Access or Collect

  • Photos and camera. With your permission, the app accesses your camera or photo library so you can capture or select images to edit. The app accesses only the images you choose.

  • Purchase status. We use Google Play Billing to confirm subscriptions or purchases. We do not receive or store your payment card number or bank details.
  • Diagnostic and usage data. We may use Firebase services to understand app performance, feature usage, crash reports, app version, device model, operating system version, language, and similar technical information.
  • Notifications. If you allow notifications, the app may send reminders or service messages related to your usage of the app. You can disable notifications in your device settings.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not require you to create an account.
  • We do not collect your real name, phone number, address, contacts, calendar, health data, or precise location.
  • We do not access your microphone.
  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not use your images or face image information to build a biometric identity profile.

How We Use Information

  • To provide AI image editing, image replacement, object removal, and related app features.
  • To process the image, mask, prompt, or face image information you submit for a requested edit.
  • To verify subscription status and provide paid features.
  • To maintain security, prevent abuse, troubleshoot errors, and improve app reliability.
  • To respond to support, privacy, or deletion requests.
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations.

AI Image and Face Processing

AI editing may be performed on secure cloud servers operated by our service providers. Images, masks, prompts, and face image information are processed only for the purpose of generating the edit you request.

We do not sell, rent, or otherwise disclose your images or face image information to third parties for advertising or profiling. We do not use this information to identify you. Where server-side processing is required, data is transmitted using encrypted connections.

Generated results are stored on your device unless you choose to save, share, or back them up elsewhere. Temporary server-side processing files are deleted after processing according to our operational retention practices and our providers' retention policies.

Images and generated results saved in the app remain on your device until you delete them or uninstall the app. We do not keep a permanent copy of your edited images on our own servers.
