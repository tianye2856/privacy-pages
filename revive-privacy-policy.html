Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026 · Version 1.0

Revive is an AI photo app that restores, repairs, colorizes, sharpens and enhances old or damaged photos. This policy explains what the app processes and how your photos are handled.

To restore a photo, the image you choose is uploaded to a secure third-party cloud AI service for processing and then returned to your device. Only photos you explicitly select are ever sent. We never sell your photos or personal information.

1. What the App Does

Revive lets you pick an old, blurry, scratched, faded, or black-and-white photo and uses AI models to repair damage, restore and enhance faces, colorize black-and-white images, unblur and upscale to higher resolution, and optionally animate a portrait. The restored result is returned to your device, where you can save or share it.

2. Information We Process

The app may process:

  • Photos you select — the specific image(s) you choose to restore, and the restored output.
  • Camera input — if you take a photo of a printed picture to restore it.
  • Device and usage data — app version, device model, operating system, language, coarse region, crash logs, and anonymous interaction events used to improve the app.
  • Subscription status — whether you hold an active subscription (handled through Apple).

We do not require your real name, email address, phone number, or an account to use the app. We do not access your full photo library — only the images you specifically pick.

3. How We Use Information

  • To restore, repair, colorize, enhance, upscale, or animate the photo you submit.
  • To return and let you save or share the result.
  • To operate, maintain, secure, and improve the app and fix problems.
  • To process subscriptions and prevent abuse or fraud.

We do not sell your photos or personal information, and we do not use your photos to train our own models for unrelated purposes.

4. AI Processing & Third Parties

Photo restoration is performed by AI models hosted on third-party cloud infrastructure (including model-hosting providers such as Replicate). When you request a restoration, the selected image and the parameters needed for that request are transmitted over an encrypted connection to the processing service, which returns the result. Images are processed to fulfill your request and are not used to identify you.

The app also uses the following service providers:

  • Cloud AI processing (e.g. Replicate-hosted models) — to perform the photo restoration you request.
  • Google Firebase / Analytics — aggregated, app usage and stability analytics.
  • Umeng (友盟+) SDK — aggregated app usage and device statistics.
  • Apple — in-app purchases and subscription management.

Each provider processes data under its own terms and privacy policy.

5. Data Storage & Retention

Restored results and app preferences may be stored locally on your device, and you can save results to your photo library at your choice. Images sent for processing are handled transiently to complete your request and are not retained by us for advertising or profiling. Local data is removed when you delete the app or clear its data.

6. Permissions

  • Photo Library — to let you choose a photo to restore and to save the result back.
  • Camera — only if you take a photo of a printed picture.

You can change these permissions anytime in your device Settings.

7. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your information. We may disclose information only when necessary to:

  • Provide the photo-processing service through our providers.
  • Comply with applicable law or valid legal process.
  • Protect the safety, rights, or integrity of users, the app, or others.

8. Children

The app is not intended for children under 13. If you are under the age required by your country or region to use digital services, you may use the app only with the involvement of a parent or legal guardian.

9. Your Choices & Rights

Because the app works without an account, the simplest way to remove app-stored data is to delete the app. Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal information; contact us and we will respond as required by applicable law.

10. Security

We use reasonable technical measures, including encrypted transmission, to protect data in transit. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
