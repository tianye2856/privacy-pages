1. Who we are

Cthulhu Jigsaw, also presented in some localized text as Abyss Summons, is an iOS jigsaw puzzle game distributed through the Apple App Store. The app's bundle identifier is com.zhouying.jigsaw.

In this policy, "we", "our", and "us" refer to the operator of Cthulhu Jigsaw. "You" means anyone who installs or uses the app.

To reach us about anything in this policy, write to zhouying381885071@gmail.com.

2. Data you give us

Game progress and settings

Cthulhu Jigsaw stores game progress, unlocked status, settings, current level state, and similar gameplay data locally on your device. This data is used so you can continue playing and keep your preferences.

Images you choose

If you choose a custom image for puzzle play, the app uses that image on your device to create the puzzle. The app does not upload your selected images to our servers, and we do not use them for advertising, analytics, or model training.

Support email

If you email us for support, we receive your email address and any information you choose to include, such as screenshots, device details, or a description of the issue. We use this only to respond to your request and improve the app.

No account: Cthulhu Jigsaw has no login system. We do not ask for your name, phone number, contacts, calendar, health data, or precise location.

3. Data collected automatically

We use analytics and diagnostics to understand how the app is used, find bugs, and improve stability.

Usage events

The app may send anonymous usage events such as app launches, screen views, puzzle progress, tool usage, sharing actions, paywall views, and purchase flow status. These events help us understand where users get stuck and which features need improvement.

Diagnostics

If the app crashes or behaves unexpectedly, we may receive basic diagnostic information such as iOS version, device model, app version, language, and the screen or feature involved.

Installation identifiers

Analytics SDKs may generate an anonymous installation or instance ID. This is not your Apple ID, phone number, or IDFA. Reinstalling the app may generate a new ID.

What we do not do: we do not use IDFA / App Tracking Transparency for advertising tracking, we do not sell personal information, and we do not track you across other companies' apps or websites.

4. Purchases

Paid unlocks are sold and processed by Apple through the App Store using StoreKit. We do not receive your credit card number, billing address, or full Apple ID account details.

Apple may provide the app with purchase status, product identifier, transaction identifier, and restore status so the app can unlock paid content. Apple's handling of payment data is governed by Apple's privacy policy: apple.com/legal/privacy.

5. Third-party services

Firebase Analytics (Google LLC)

Firebase Analytics is used for anonymous usage events and diagnostics as described above. Data may be processed on Google infrastructure in the United States and other regions. Privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy.

Umeng+ Analytics

The app may use Umeng+ analytics, operated by Umeng (Beijing) Information Technology Co., Ltd., to report anonymous usage events and basic device information. Events sent to Umeng+ do not include your custom puzzle images. Privacy policy: umeng.com/page/policy.

Apple StoreKit

Apple StoreKit is used for in-app purchases and restore purchase status. See Section 4.

6. How long we keep data

  • On your device. Game progress, settings, and selected custom images remain on your device until you delete them, reset progress, clear images, or remove the app.
  • Support messages. Emails are kept as long as reasonably needed to respond to you and maintain support records.
  • Analytics events. Firebase and Umeng+ retain analytics data according to their own settings and policies.
  • Purchase records. Apple retains App Store transaction records according to its own policies. The app may keep local purchase status so paid content remains unlocked.

To exercise these rights, contact zhouying381885071@gmail.com. Because the app has no account system, we may ask for information needed to locate relevant records.

EU / EEA / UK residents (GDPR / UK GDPR). The legal bases for processing are performance of the contract under which we provide the app and paid unlocks, our legitimate interest in maintaining and improving the app, and your consent where required by law.

If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

10. Security

No system is perfectly secure. If we discover a breach affecting your personal information, we will notify you and relevant authorities as required by law.
