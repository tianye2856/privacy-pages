Getting started

How do I play?

  • Open the Levels tab and choose a case.
  • Read the brief and evidence cards carefully.
  • Answer each deduction question by matching the clue chain.
  • Lock the final suspect to see your score and the full explanation.

What should I do if I am stuck?

Each deduction can reveal a hint. Hints are optional and reduce your final score, but they can help you understand which evidence matters.

Progress and reset

Progress is stored locally on your device. To start over, open Settings in the app and use the reset option. Deleting the app also removes local progress.

Languages

CaseCrack includes English, Spanish, German, Simplified Chinese, and Brazilian Portuguese App Store metadata support. In-app case content can be switched from the Settings tab where available.

Offline play

The current version works offline and does not require an account, login, subscription, or internet connection to play the bundled cases.

Purchases and refunds

The current version does not include in-app purchases or subscriptions. If purchases are added in a future version, they will be processed by Apple through your Apple ID and refund requests will be handled by Apple at reportaproblem.apple.com.

Troubleshooting

  • Make sure your device is running a supported iOS version.
  • Restart the app if a screen appears stuck.
  • Update to the latest App Store version when available.
  • Use the in-app reset option only if you want to clear local progress.

Privacy and terms

CaseCrack does not require an account and does not collect data in the current version. See the full Privacy Policy and Terms of Use.
