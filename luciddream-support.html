Help for dream journaling, AI interpretations, cultural lenses, subscriptions, and privacy in Lucid Dream Atlas.

Contact us

Email feedback@globalpixelstudio.com for support, privacy questions, subscription issues, or feature requests.

Using Lucid Dream Atlas

  • Write your dream as soon as you remember it.
  • Choose an emotion label and interpretation lens.
  • Review the AI interpretation as a reflection aid, not as a prediction.
  • Use the journal and insights areas to notice repeating images and themes.

AI Interpretations

Interpretations may draw on cultural, symbolic, and psychological perspectives. They can be incomplete or unsuitable for your circumstances. Do not use them as medical, psychological, religious, legal, financial, or other professional advice.

Subscriptions

Plus and Pro are optional monthly subscriptions handled by Apple. You can manage or cancel them anytime in your Apple ID settings:

  • Open Settings on your iPhone.
  • Tap your Apple ID name.
  • Tap Subscriptions.
  • Select Lucid Dream Atlas and manage the plan.

Refund requests are handled by Apple at reportaproblem.apple.com.

Privacy

Read the full Privacy Policy & Terms of Use. Please avoid entering passwords, government ID numbers, financial account details, health records, or other highly sensitive information into your dream text.

Troubleshooting

  • Make sure your device is connected to the internet when requesting an AI interpretation.
  • If products do not load, check your App Store account and try again later.
  • If the app behaves unexpectedly, restart the app and confirm you are using the latest version from the App Store.
