Getting started

How do I identify a mushroom?

  • Open the app and tap the camera button.
  • Take a clear photo of the mushroom, or choose an existing one from your library.
  • The app sends that one photo for AI analysis and shows you the most likely matches.

Tips for a better result

  • Use good lighting and get close, keeping the mushroom in focus.
  • Capture the cap, the gills/pores underneath, and the stem where possible.
  • Avoid blurry, dark, or cluttered photos with several different mushrooms.

How accurate is identification?

Results are AI-generated suggestions and probabilities only. They can be incomplete or wrong, especially for look-alike species. Treat every result as a starting point for further research — never as confirmation that a mushroom is safe. See our Terms of Use for the full disclaimer.

Subscriptions & billing

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are billed through your Apple ID. Manage or cancel any time in Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions on your iPhone, or at apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions. Cancel at least 24 hours before the renewal date to avoid the next charge. Deleting the app does not cancel a subscription.

How do I restore a purchase?

If you reinstalled the app or switched devices, open the paywall and tap Restore Purchases while signed in with the same Apple ID you used to subscribe.

How do I request a refund?

All purchases are processed by Apple, so refunds are handled by Apple at reportaproblem.apple.com. We aren't able to issue App Store refunds directly.

Permissions

Camera & photos

The app asks for camera or photo access only so you can capture or pick a mushroom photo to identify, and for permission to save a result. You can change these any time in Settings → Mushroom ID on your iPhone.

Privacy & your data

Mushroom ID has no account or login. Your photos stay on your device unless you start an identification, and we don't keep a copy of them on our servers. To remove all local data, simply delete the app. Full details are in our Privacy Policy.

Referral codes

If you have an invite or referral code, you can enter it during onboarding. Codes are optional and aren't tied to your identity.
